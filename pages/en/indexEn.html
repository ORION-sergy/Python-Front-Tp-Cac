GAME IN DEVELOPMENT

"The Path Into The Abyss" is a first-person shooter where you will have to survive in an Argentina immersed in the apocalypse.

What is the abyss?

"The abyss" is the City of Buenos Aires, so called because it is ground zero, a city in ruins transformed by invaders of unknown origin that you must face.

Open World

This is a game where you will can move freely around the world and chart your own adventure. You will be able to explore cities, neighborhoods, routes, forests and the Argentine countryside, each place with its own ecosystem.

Difficulty

The game aims for extreme difficulty and there is no map, so you will have to trust your instincts and navigate carefully.

Dynamic NPC's

You'll find NPC's in The Abyss and it's surroundings, each one with a story to tell. You can make a travel companion out of them, turn into alies or even enemies.

We hope you enjoy it!
